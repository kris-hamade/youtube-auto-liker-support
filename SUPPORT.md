# Support

Need help, found a bug, or have a feature request?

Please open a support request here:

https://github.com/kris-hamade/youtube-auto-liker-support/issues

When reporting a bug, please include:

- What you expected to happen
- What actually happened
- Your Chrome version
- Extension version
- Whether the issue happens on all YouTube videos or only certain channels

Please do not include personal information, private YouTube account details, or sensitive browser data.

## Before Opening An Issue

Please check the existing issues and FAQ first:

- Issues: https://github.com/kris-hamade/youtube-auto-liker-support/issues
- FAQ: ./FAQ.md

## Response Time

Support requests are reviewed as time allows. Clear reports with steps to reproduce are the easiest to investigate.
