# Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 9, 2026

YouTube Auto Liker is designed to run in your browser and help automate liking YouTube videos based on the extension's settings.

## Information Collected

YouTube Auto Liker does not sell personal information.

The extension may store settings locally in your browser so it can remember your preferences. These settings may include extension configuration values, such as whether the extension is enabled and how it should behave on YouTube pages.

## Information Not Collected

YouTube Auto Liker does not intentionally collect:

- Your name
- Your email address
- Your YouTube password
- Private YouTube account details
- Payment information
- Sensitive browser data unrelated to the extension's function

## How Information Is Used

Locally stored settings are used only to provide the extension's functionality and remember your preferences.

## Third-Party Services

YouTube Auto Liker runs on YouTube pages. Your use of YouTube is governed by Google's and YouTube's own terms and privacy policies.

This support repository is hosted by GitHub. If you open an issue or comment here, that content is handled by GitHub according to GitHub's policies.

## Support Requests

When opening a support request, do not include personal information, private YouTube account details, or sensitive browser data.

## Changes To This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted in this repository.

## Contact

For support or privacy questions, please open an issue:

https://github.com/kris-hamade/youtube-auto-liker-support/issues
